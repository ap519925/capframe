# Privacy Policy for Capframe

**Last updated:** December 17, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Capframe** ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our desktop screen recording application.

## 2. Information Collection and Use

**Capframe** is designed as a **local-first** desktop application.

*   **Screen Recordings**: All video and audio recordings created with Capframe are processed and stored locally on your device. We do not upload your recordings to our servers or any cloud storage automatically.
*   **Personal Data**: We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
*   **Usage Data**: We do not collect anonymous usage data or analytics.

## 3. AI Features

Capframe includes AI-powered features for video analysis and smart trimming.
*   Currently, all AI processing logic is performed or simulated locally within the application.
*   No video content is transmitted to external servers for AI analysis.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The application provides convenient links to share your content on third-party platforms (such as YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, and Discord).
*   When you use these features, you are interacting directly with those third-party services.
*   We do not have access to your accounts or login credentials for these services.
*   Please review the privacy policies of these third-party platforms to understand how they handle your data.

## 5. Security

We value your trust. Since your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your recordings relies on the security of your computer. We recommend keeping your operating system and security software up to date.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in this file or on our future website.
